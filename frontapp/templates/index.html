Our Services

WorldWide Tours

World Wide Tours & Travels is a travel agency that specializes in group travel to exciting destinations around the world. They offer services for all travel needs and different types of groups.

Hotel Reservation

A hotel reservation system, also known as a hotel booking engine or hotel booking software, allows users to make reservations through a website or other channels. Travel agencies and operators use these systems to display and sell their hotel inventory, and customers can use the system to choose options.

Travel Guides

A travel guide can include information on the various sites, hotels, restaurants, transportation, and activities that may be of interest to a visitor. Sometimes known as guidebooks, travel guides can also include information on a country's culture, currency, and language for international travelers.

Event Management

Event management and planning can have a significant impact on local tourism. By organising and hosting events, hotels and restaurants can attract more visitors to their area. This can lead to a boom in local tourism, as well as an increase in revenue for businesses in the hospitality industry.

I am a bit old school because I always carry a tiny notebook when I travel. I use this to note where we ate, what we ate, where we stayed, attractions we visited, etc. This way, I have more details for writing my travel reviews before I forget. Typing or taking notes on your smartphone will work just as well.

John Abraham

New York, USA

How long your travel review is, is entirely up to you. However, one line is not worth posting. There wont be enough context to be of use to anyone. Too long, and people may not get through it all due to short attention spans. Use complete sentences with enough detail to describe your travel experiences well.

New York, USA

I always indicate in my travel reviews the particular day (and time, if necessary) that I visited the establishment. I also post images of the receipt for restaurants so they can identify servers, tables, items ordered etc.
